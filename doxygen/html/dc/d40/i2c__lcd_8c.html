implementation of functions for printing strings via I2C to 2x16 LCD Batron BTHQ21605V-COG-FSRE-I2C (Farnell 1220409). Connect LCD I2C bus to STM8 SCL/SDA, and LCD reset pin to STM8 pin PE3
